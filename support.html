Common Questions

How do I get started with OZMAN? ▼
Download OZMAN from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android), and start your 7-day free trial. No credit card required. You can set up your first project in under a minute.
Where is my data stored? ▼
All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your construction project data on any server. Your data is 100% yours and stays on your phone.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID Account Settings (iOS) or Google Play Subscriptions (Android). No cancellation fees or penalties.
What happens to my data if I cancel? ▼
Since all data is stored locally on your device, it stays on your phone even after cancelling. You can also use the Backup & Restore feature to export your data as a JSON file before uninstalling.
How do I back up my data? ▼
Go to Settings in the app and tap "Backup & Restore". You can export all your data as a JSON file and save it wherever you like (iCloud, Google Drive, email, etc.). You can restore from this backup at any time.
Is OZMAN available on both iPhone and Android? ▼
Yes. OZMAN is available on the Apple App Store for iPhone and iPad, and on the Google Play Store for Android devices.
Do you offer team accounts? ▼
Currently, OZMAN is designed for individual users. Team features are on our roadmap. Contact us at Info.zecongroup@gmail.com if you're interested in team collaboration.
How much does OZMAN Pro cost? ▼
OZMAN Pro is $12.99 AUD per month with a 7-day free trial. No credit card required to start. Cancel anytime through your Apple ID or Google Play account settings.
